      • Material

  • 5 chicken thighs
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons of tamarind water
  • 6 tablespoons of honey
  • 350 ml of coconut water
  • 1 lemongrass stalk (geprek)
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons of Javanese sugar (combed)
  • to taste pepper and salt

      • Ground spices

  • 5 cayenne peppers
  • 5 cloves of shallot
  • 3 cloves of garlic

      • Spread

  • 6 tablespoons of honey
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon of stir-fried broth

      • How To Make

  • Wash chicken thoroughly, coat chicken with pepper, salt and 3 tablespoons of tamarind water, soak for about 15 minutes.
  • Saute ground spices with butter, add lemongrass, bay leaves and palm sugar, saute until fragrant. Add chicken that has been soaked in coconut water, cook until the water has shrunk, and tender chicken meat.
